Our Mission

Bringing Clarity to Those Who Need It Most

In East Africa, thousands of children and elderly suffer from preventable blindness and vision impairment due to lack of access to basic eye care. We bridge this gap by bringing mobile clinics directly to orphanages, schools, religious centers, and communities.

Free Eye Screenings

Comprehensive vision tests for all ages using modern portable equipment

Prescription Glasses

Custom-fitted eyewear distributed on-site at no cost to recipients

Cataract Surgeries

Life-changing operations arranged for severe cases in partner hospitals
